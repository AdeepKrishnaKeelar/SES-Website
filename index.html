About Us

Started in 1969, carrying forward the visions of selfless individuals who wanted to promote education, today the 52 year old Sampaje Composite PU and High School helps to provide the right education for the students who aim to build a career in the world.

Sampaje Composite PU College provides education in the field of Commerce and Arts and enjoys a very good percentage every year while the Sampaje High School provides the prescribed SSLC board syllabus and also enjoys a very good record. Under the guidance of Shri. KG Rajaram Keelar and his highly experienced team of various backgrounds, the Institute continues to enjoy great results.
